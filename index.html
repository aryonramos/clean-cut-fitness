About Me

I Didn't Get
Into Fitness
for the Obvious
Reasons.

I didn't get into fitness because I had some dramatic transformation. I got into it because I needed something that was mine.

Growing up, sport was always my outlet. But when I left high school, the teams stopped, the structure disappeared, and I had no real direction. The gym was the one thing I kept coming back to — not because I had a goal at first, but because it gave me something to focus on when everything else felt uncertain.

Over nearly 5 years of consistent training, what started as just showing up turned into something bigger. It built my confidence. It gave me discipline. It helped me figure out who I actually was outside of the person I used to be.

That's why I started Clean Cut Fitness. Because I know what it's like to be in decent shape on the outside but feel completely lost on the inside. If you're a guy who's ready to take that step — not just in the gym, but in life — that's exactly who I'm here for.

Aryon Ramos
Founder, Clean Cut Fitness
